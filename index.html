Akhilesh Gotmare

I am a Machine Learning Researcher with Salesforce Research in Singapore where I work on deep learning and its natural language processing applications. Previously, I completed my MSc at the Department of Computer Science at EPFL, Switzerland, where I was working with Prof. Martin Jaggi's Machine Learning and Optimization laboratory for my thesis project on model parallel training of deep neural nets.

Prior to EPFL, I completed my undergraduate studies in Electrical Engineering (with a CSE minor) from IIT Gandhinagar, where among other things I had the fun experience of organizing the maiden TEDx event on campus!
