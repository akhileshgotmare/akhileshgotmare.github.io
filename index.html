Akhilesh Gotmare

I am an AI Researcher with Salesforce Research in Singapore where I work on deep learning and its applications in processing natural language and code. My current focus is on building LLM Agents and workflows to improve the software development cycle.

I lead AI research on ApexGuru—Salesforceâ€™s AI-powered code review tool that is trusted by thousands of enterprise customers to optimize and maintain their business-critical CRM applications. ApexGuru automatically flags anti-patterns and recommends LLM-generated fixes, and has been showcased in Dreamforce's Developer Keynote sessions for the past three consecutive years ('23 , '24 , '25 )

I completed my MSc at the Department of Computer Science at EPFL, Switzerland, where I was working with Prof. Martin Jaggi's Machine Learning and Optimization laboratory for my thesis project on model parallel training of deep neural nets. Prior to EPFL, I completed my undergraduate studies in Electrical Engineering (with a CSE minor) from IIT Gandhinagar, where among other things I had the fun experience of organizing the maiden TEDx event on campus!
